Welcome to Bay Shore Lutheran Church

A community of faith where all are welcome. We gather to worship, learn, and serve together in Christ's love.

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture faith through Word and Sacrament, and serve our neighbors in love.

Our Values

We value faithfulness to Scripture, reverent worship, genuine fellowship, compassionate service, and the proclamation of God's grace

Our Vision

To be a growing community of faith where all are welcomed, lives are transformed by the Gospel, and God's love is shared with the world.

All Are Welcome!

Everyone is welcome at Bay Shore Lutheran Church! We are a community that embraces all who seek to know God. We are grounded in Biblical truth and the teachings of Christ, which guide our faith and fellowship.
